## ğŸ“± How to Use

### For Members: Sharing Your Testimony

#### Step 1: Access Testimony Vault
1. Open the app and login
2. Tap **"Testimony Vault"** on the Home screen (purple icon)

#### Step 2: Submit Your Testimony
1. Tap the **"Share Testimony"** floating button
2. Choose your format:
   - **Text**: Type your testimony
   - **Audio**: Upload audio recording (MP3, M4A, etc.)
   - **Video**: Upload video (MP4, max 100MB)

#### Step 3: Fill in Details
- **Category**: Select the category that best fits
- **Title**: Give your testimony a catchy title
- **Content/Description**:
  - For text: Write your full testimony
  - For audio/video: Brief description

#### Step 4: Submit
- Tap **"Submit Testimony"**
- Status: **Pending** (awaiting admin approval)
- You'll be notified when approved

### For Admins: Managing Testimonies

#### Viewing Pending Testimonies
1. Go to Admin Panel
2. Select **"Pending Testimonies"**
3. Review submissions

#### Approving Testimonies
1. Read/watch/listen to the testimony
2. Choose action:
   - âœ… **Approve**: Make it public
   - â­ **Approve & Feature**: Highlight it
   - âŒ **Reject**: With optional reason
3. Tap **"Approve"** or **"Reject"**

#### Featuring Testimonies
- Select an approved testimony
- Toggle **"Featured"** status
- Featured testimonies appear at the top
