## ðŸ“± How to Use

### For Members: Submitting a Prayer Request

#### Step 1: Access Prayer Wall
1. Open the app and login
2. Tap **"Prayer Wall"** on the Home screen
3. You'll see tabs: Public | Private | Leadership (if applicable) | Answered

#### Step 2: Submit Your Prayer
1. Tap the **"Add Prayer"** floating button
2. Fill in the details:
   - **Title**: Brief, clear title (e.g., "Healing for Mom")
   - **Prayer Request**: Full details of what you need prayer for
   - **Category**: Select the category that fits best
   - **Privacy Level**:
     - Public: Everyone prays
     - Private: Only leadership sees it
     - Leadership: Pastors only

#### Step 3: Optional Settings
- âœ… **Mark as Urgent**: For immediate, critical needs
- âœ… **Submit Anonymously**: Your name won't be shown

#### Step 4: Submit & Trust
- Tap **"Submit Prayer Request"**
- Your prayer immediately goes to the Prayer Wall
- Church members are notified
- You can see who's praying for you

### For Members: Praying for Others

#### How to Pray
1. Browse the Prayer Wall (Public/Private/Leadership tabs)
2. Tap on any prayer request to read details
3. Pray right there
4. Tap **"I Prayed for This"** button
5. The requester sees you prayed!

#### Filter Prayers
- Tap the **filter icon** (top right)
- Choose a specific category
- Focus your prayers on areas where God is calling you

### For Prayer Owners: Marking as Answered

#### When God Answers
1. Go to **Your Prayers** or find the prayer in the wall
2. Open the prayer detail
3. Tap the **3-dot menu** â†’ **"Mark as Answered"**
4. Share your testimony: How did God answer?
5. Submit

#### Convert to Public Testimony
After marking as answered, you'll be asked:
> "Would you like to share this as a testimony?"

Choose **"Yes, Share"** to:
- Encourage others facing similar situations
- Give glory to God publicly
- Build faith in the congregation

(The testimony goes through admin approval before being public)
